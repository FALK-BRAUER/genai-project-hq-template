## What It Does
Run a solo AI consulting practice with the systems of a 10-person firm. Covers the full operational backbone of your-project: client lifecycle management, sales process, client onboarding, bookkeeping, Singapore tax compliance, time tracking with utilization metrics, and revenue planning with diversification roadmap.

Key sections:
- Client management (communication cadence, scope management, difficult client playbook)
- Sales (discovery call script, proposal template, rate card, objection handling, follow-up sequence)
- Client onboarding (checklist, welcome pack, kickoff call script)
- Bookkeeping (monthly P&L, expense categories, invoice template, GST tracking, cash flow)
- Tax (SG corporate tax overview, calendar, Form C-S eligibility, CPF rates, deductible expenses)
- Time tracking (utilization framework targeting 50-60% billable, weekly audit template, priority matrix)
- Revenue planning (stream audit, mix targets by stage, service-to-product ladder, evaluation scorecard)

All adapted for Singapore context with SGD pricing, IRAS/CPF/MOM compliance, and SG market rate benchmarks.

## Tips & Gotchas
- Billable utilization target is 50-60% of a 40-hour week (20-24 billable hours).
- Never chase passive income before your core consulting offer is proven.
- Minimum cash buffer: 3 months of operating expenses.
- After 5 follow-up touches with no response, move to nurture list. Do not chase.
- Set aside 10-15% of revenue in a dedicated account for tax.
