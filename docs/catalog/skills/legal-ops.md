## What It Does
Singapore-first legal operations and compliance skill for an AI consulting practice. Covers contract review, statutory compliance, data protection, IP, and legal spend tracking with multi-jurisdiction support (US, EU, UK).

Key sections:
- Contract review checklist (11 key clauses to verify with red flags, 9 NDA-specific automatic checks, negotiation points)
- Compliance calendar (ACRA annual return, IRAS ECI/Form C-S, GST returns, CPF contributions, PDPA review, insurance renewals) with RAG-status escalation timeline
- PDPA compliance (all 9 data protection obligations, mandatory breach notification within 3 calendar days, cross-border transfer rules, DPO appointment guidance, data protection policy template)
- IP protection (work product vs background IP assignment rules, open source license compliance hierarchy from MIT/BSD through GPL/AGPL, AI model ownership considerations, trade secret protection)
- Legal spend tracking (budget categories with typical costs, hourly vs fixed fee decision matrix, matter management tracking, anomaly detection flags)
- Escalation rules for contracts >S$50,000, data breaches, regulatory inquiries, litigation threats

Also covers Singapore legal system overview, director obligations under the Companies Act, and multi-jurisdiction notes for US/EU/UK clients.

## Tips & Gotchas
- ALL outputs require review by licensed attorney. This skill provides legal analysis, not legal advice.
- PDPA breach notification deadline is 3 calendar days -- never miss this.
- Never assign background IP to a client without explicit director approval.
- Any GPL/AGPL component in a proprietary client deliverable is an automatic escalation to IP counsel.
- For contracts >S$50,000, always get external legal counsel to review before signing.
