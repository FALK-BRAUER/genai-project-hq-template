## What It Does
Small business accounting for a Singapore-based consulting firm. Covers chart of accounts, monthly P&L, expense tracking, GST compliance, cash flow forecasting, and budget variance analysis. Configured for Singapore tax regime (17% corporate tax, 9% GST, CPF rates) with SGD as primary currency and USD for international invoicing.

Includes:
- Chart of accounts (revenue, COGS, opex, tax accounts)
- Monthly P&L template with margin targets
- IRAS-deductible expense categories (Section 14, S14 R&D, capital allowances)
- GST tracking and quarterly return template
- 13-week rolling cash flow forecast
- Budget variance analysis with flagging rules
- Singapore tax calendar with annual rhythm

## Tips & Gotchas
- This is for management accounting and planning, not audit-grade reporting. Get a qualified accountant for statutory filing.
- Templates assume accrual basis accounting (recognize revenue when earned, not when cash received).
- Keep receipts for 5 years (IRAS requirement).
- If voluntary GST registration with >90% zero-rated revenue (overseas clients), you can claim back input tax on local expenses.
- Flag any budget variance >10% AND >S$500 to avoid noise from small-value items.
