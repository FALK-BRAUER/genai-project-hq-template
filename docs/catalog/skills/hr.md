## What It Does
HR essentials reference for a 1-2 person Singapore consulting firm. Covers the rules you need to not get fined under the Singapore Employment Act.

Key sections:
- Employment contract essentials (mandatory Key Employment Terms within 14 days, standard clauses for probation, notice, non-compete, IP assignment, confidentiality)
- CPF contributions (17% employer / 20% employee rates, OW ceiling S$7,400/month, due by 14th of following month)
- MOM compliance and work passes (EP minimum salary S$5,600/month, COMPASS framework, S Pass quotas, MyCareersFuture advertising requirements)
- Leave entitlements (annual leave 7-14 days by seniority, sick leave 14+60 days, maternity 16 weeks, paternity 2 weeks, childcare 6 days)
- WICA insurance (mandatory for manual workers and non-manual employees earning at most S$2,600/month)
- New hire onboarding checklist (before start date, first week, first month)
- Key dates calendar (CPF 14th monthly, IR8A by 1 March, EP renewal 6 months before expiry)

## Tips & Gotchas
- Every employee must receive written Key Employment Terms (KETs) within 14 days of starting.
- Never let an employee start work before their work pass is approved -- IPA alone is not sufficient.
- CPF late payment attracts 1.5% interest per month plus possible court action.
- WICA insurance must be in place before the employee starts work. Penalty for non-compliance: fine up to S$10,000 and/or 12 months imprisonment.
- For departing foreign employees, file IR21 tax clearance within 1 month and withhold pay until IRAS clears.
